Right Click (Hold): Camera rotation/inclination
Right Click (Double Click): Set camera center point on cursor position
Middle Mouse Scroll: Camera zoom
Middle Mouse (Hold): Camera Position
Middle Mouse (Double Click): Reset camera position
Left/Right Arrows: Camera rotation
Left/Right Arrows + Left Ctrl (Hold): Slow camera rotation
Up/Down Arrows: Camera Inclination
Up/Down Arrows + Left Ctrl (Hold): Slow camera inclination
Up/Down/Left/Right + Left Shift (Hold): Camera position
